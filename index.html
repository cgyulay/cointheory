Notes about NVT Ratio

NVT Ratio (Network Value to Transactions Ratio) is similar to the PE Ratio found in equity valuation.

A high NVT indicates that the cryptoasset's on-chain transaction volume is valued more highly. This generally means that the network is experiencing rapid growth and its future returns are valued more highly by the market, or that there is a potential bubble in price if this increased valuation is unfounded.

Our project is to try to better separate these situations through better analysis of transaction activity and fundamental drivers.

NVT is calculated by the network value (total market capitalization) divided by the transaction volume (in dollars) on the blockchain. The transaction volume is only an estimate derived from activity seen on blockchains. All data is gathered from block explorers by Coin Metrics.
